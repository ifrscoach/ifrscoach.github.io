Data & Account Deletion

We respect your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data (including learning progress and notes) permanently, you have two options:

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Instant)

You can delete your account instantly from within the app:

  1. Open the IFRS Coach app.
  2. Go to My Account (Profile Icon).
  3. Scroll down to the "Session" section.
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

Option 2: Request via Email

If you have uninstalled the app or cannot access your account, you may request deletion via email. Please send an email from the address associated with your account.
