Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to IFRS Coach. This application is developed and maintained by Mustafa Sharara. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Data We Collect

We collect specific data to provide account synchronization, progress tracking, and premium features.

a. Account Information

If you choose to create an account, we collect your email address and name via Firebase Authentication (email/password, Google Sign-In, or Apple Sign-In). This allows you to log in across multiple devices and recover your account.

b. User-Generated Content

We store your learning progress, bookmarks, notes, and profile preferences (such as avatar choice and preferred language) securely on our cloud servers (Google Firestore) so that your data remains synced across your devices.

c. Log Data & Usage

In a case of an error in the app, we collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

d. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Link to privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the app:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics

3. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely for:

  • Account Sync: Allowing you to access your notes and progress on any device.
  • App Stability: Improving performance and fixing crashes (Crashlytics).
  • Feature Development: Understanding user engagement to build better tools (Analytics).
  • Ad Support: Serving relevant advertisements to support the free version of the app (AdMob).

4. Data Storage and Security

Cloud Storage: Your account data and progress are stored securely using Google Cloud Firestore, which employs industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest.

Security: We value your trust in providing us your information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

5. Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do the necessary actions.

6. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You have full control over your data. You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time. Upon deletion, your account, email, notes, and progress will be permanently removed from our servers.

  • Immediate In-App Deletion: You can delete your account instantly by navigating to Profile > Delete Account within the app.
  • Deletion Request by Email: If you cannot access the app, you may request data deletion by emailing us at support@ifrsapp.zohodesk.eu with the subject line "Delete Account". We will process your request within 30 days.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of personalized ads and analytics tracking via the "Data & Privacy Settings" menu inside the app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: support@ifrsapp.zohodesk.eu
