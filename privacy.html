Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to IFRS Coach. This application is developed and maintained by Mustafa Sharara. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Data We Collect

We strive to collect the minimum amount of data necessary to operate this app.

a. Personal Information

We do NOT collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number directly within the app. All user-generated content (such as notes, bookmarks, and quiz progress) is stored locally on your device.

b. Log Data & Usage

In a case of an error in the app, we collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

c. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Link to privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the app:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics

3. How We Use Your Data

The anonymous data we collect is used solely for:

  • Improving app stability and performance (Crashlytics).
  • Understanding user engagement to develop new features (Analytics).
  • Serving relevant advertisements to support the free version of the app (AdMob).

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Data: Your personal progress, notes, and bookmarks are stored securely on your device. If you uninstall the app, this data may be lost unless you have backed up your device.

Security: We value your trust in providing us your information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

5. Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do the necessary actions.

6. Your Rights

  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of personalized ads and analytics tracking via the "Data & Privacy Settings" menu.
  • Deletion: Since we do not store your personal data on our servers, we cannot provide a copy or delete it. You can delete your local data by uninstalling the app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
